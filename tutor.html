Tutorial Classes

- I provide ONLY Mathematics/Computer Science Tutorials (Gr6 - Gr12), including Functions, Calculus and Data Management

- Tutorial classes will include finishing an 3 page exercises designed by me, syllabus will corresponding to the student's progress and syllabus at school.

- First part of the exercise would be a quick review of what the student was learning in the previous class.

- The latter part would be advanced problems on the same topic and/or teaching a new topic.

- I recommend having 2-3 classes of the same subject every week because only by systematic training can students be fully prepared and confident for Math Exams.

- New students may contact to try out a class (1 hour) for free.

- Referrals are also welcome.

- For every successful referral, you may get a free class (1.5 hr)!
